Who am I?

Jinpeng Wang (王锦鹏) is a Researcher in Knowledge Computing Group at Microsoft Research Asia. He received his Ph.D. in Jun. 2016 from Peking University. His research interests broadly include: natural language processing, information retrieval and deep learning. He is currently working on research topics related to text generation.

F.Nietzsche
