Who am I?

Jinpeng Wang (王锦鹏) is a Ph.D. student in NC&IS, School of Electronics Engineer and Computer Science, Peking University, China. I'm a member of the Search Engine and Web Mining (SEWM) Research Group. My Research interests broadly include: social media content analysis, wext mining, NLP and recommendation system.
